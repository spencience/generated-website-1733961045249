Volunteer Opportunities

Make a Difference in Penguin Conservation

Join our dedicated team of volunteers and contribute to the preservation of penguin species worldwide. We offer various opportunities for individuals passionate about penguin conservation.

Current Opportunities

  • Field Research Assistant

    Help collect data on penguin populations in Antarctica and sub-Antarctic islands.

  • Education Program Guide

    Lead educational tours and workshops about penguin conservation.

  • Habitat Restoration

    Participate in coastal cleanup and habitat improvement projects.

  • Virtual Volunteer

    Assist with social media, data analysis, and online outreach.

How to Apply

Ready to join our team? Follow these steps:

  1. Complete our online volunteer application form
  2. Attend a virtual orientation session
  3. Choose your preferred volunteer program
  4. Complete required training
