Learn More About Penguins

Fascinating Penguin Facts

  • There are 18 different species of penguins, all living in the Southern Hemisphere
  • Emperor Penguins can dive up to 565 meters deep and hold their breath for up to 20 minutes
  • Penguins' black and white coloring is a form of camouflage called countershading
  • Some penguin species form lifelong bonds with their mates

Conservation Status

Many penguin species are currently facing threats from:

  • Climate change affecting their habitats
  • Ocean pollution and plastic waste
  • Overfishing reducing their food sources
  • Human disturbance in nesting areas
