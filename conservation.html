Penguin Conservation

Current Conservation Status

Of the 18 penguin species worldwide, 10 are currently listed as vulnerable or endangered. Climate change, ocean pollution, and habitat loss pose significant threats to penguin populations globally.

Major Threats

  • Climate change affecting ice coverage and food availability
  • Ocean pollution and plastic waste
  • Commercial fishing depleting prey species
  • Habitat destruction and human interference
  • Oil spills and marine accidents
